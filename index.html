On The House

Our Story

Petal & Bean started as a small flower stand on the corner of 5th and Birch. So many regulars asked for coffee that in 2019, we added an espresso bar right next to the flower counter. We still buy blooms from the same growers we started with, and every cup comes with a little flower tucked into the sleeve — just because.
